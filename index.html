A Reimagining of the Collective

What is ChipSwax?

Over the years, I've developed a network of talented connections and teams for creative projects. ChipSwax is a public mapping of these connections and a host for its content.

ChipSwax Network is invite-only and tightly-knit. We have netlabels and web shows, code projects and art trades, you get the picture. We keep ourselves busy.

ChipSwax exists to serve as a portfolio website people within our groups, to show the potential in my connections to businesses and other prospective clients.

Despite the website's recent conception, my connections have been developing talents for years. This very website is a collaborative effort.

That's enough talk. We organize ourselves according to skills. Our developed works contain our completed projects and upcoming content. Our groups show how we interact and work together.
