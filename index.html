What is ChipSwax?

Over the years, I've developed a network of friends with skills and talents along with teams and groups for creative projects. ChipSwax is a public mapping of these connections and a host for its content.

The ChipSwax Network is invite-only and tightly-knit as a group. It's a container for projects of all mediums and styles. We have netlabels and web shows, code projects and art trades, you get the picture. We keep ourselves busy.

But the main reason that ChipSwax exists is to serve as a sort of portfolio profile website for the artists, musicians, and other people within our groups. I made ChipSwax to show the potential in the talents of my connections to businesses and other prospective clients. Exposure is the key term, here.

Despite the website's recent conception, my connections have been developing talents for years, working towards a stable living with the use of their talents, if not become entirely indepedent with them through means of employment.

That's enough talk. We organize ourselves according to skills. Our developed works contain our completed projects and upcoming content. Our groups show how we interact and work together.
